Did You Know-Mythical Beast Facts:

1. In Greek Mythology, the Chimera is a fire-breathing creature with the parts of multiple animals, usually depicted as having a lion's head, a goat's body and a serpent's tail.

2.In Japanese Folklore, kitsune are intelligent foxes with magical abilities. They can shapeshift into human form and are often associated with the deity inari.
